Parapara Renovation

Previous Next

This original 1970 seaside bach effused retro charm but after a well-earned upgrade, designed by the new owners Matthes & Mascha Bender, & the renovation completed by Frank Byrne Builders in 2018, gave a new lease on life to this very special ‘get away’. An almost complete strip out of the existing rooms to revamp the 2 bedrooms, create a large open plan kitchen, dining & lounge area, a second bathroom & a new extension to the rear which includes a 4 bed bunk room, tv/chill-out room, plus a new laundry area. This bach is now modern throughout in a beachy, relaxed fashion & yet still retains the ambience & atmosphere created nearly 50 years ago by its original owners.

The newly designed steps, decking & railing at the front complete the modernisation of this very special bach, allowing further enjoyment of the stunning Bay views. Perfect for the family to holiday away from home & also the holiday tenants that attest to its popularity on holidayhouses.co.nz Frank Byrne Builders is proud to have been involved in this project along with Matthes & Mascha's vision, to bring this much loved bach to life again for future generations.
