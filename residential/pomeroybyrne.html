This beautiful, contemporary home is built in rural Golden Bay, on a slightly elevated site that lends itself to maximizing the all day sun. The high exterior angles and concrete block feature walls carry through to the interior. The tall windows and polished concrete floors and concrete block internal wall, allow for passive heating of the home, aided by underfloor heating when required. The plywood ceilings, timber floors and woodburner in the living area adds to the ambiance of the home.
