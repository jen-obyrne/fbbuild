Rural Projects

Our rural projects include new cowsheds - rotary and herringbone and all sizes of farm sheds.
Find out more by viewing our builds below.

In recent years, we have built several milking sheds in the Golden Bay area including Maratoa Farm at Hamama, Sowmans Farm at Uruwhenua, Waingaro Dairies Ltd at Hamama and Pomeroy-Byrne Farm at Kotinga. Two sheds are herring-bone style and the other two are rotary sheds, catering for herds of 300 to 600+ cows.
Along with these projects, Frank Byrne-Builders has developed a strong reputation for delivering on time and on budget, which has resulted in a loyal clientele within the farming fraternity.
